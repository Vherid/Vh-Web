Lemon Bars

1 stick unsalted butter 2 large eggs
1/4 C confectioner's surgar 1/3 C fresh lemon juice
1 C all purpose flour 1 t grated lemon zest
1/8 T salt 2 t all purpose flour
1 C granulated white sugar

Prep

Preheat oven to 350F. Butter or spray with a non stick, an 8x8 inch pan.

Crust

Mix butter and sugar until creamy and smooth. Add flour and salt, and mix until dough starts to come together. Press onto bottom of your pan, bake for 18-20 minutes, or until lightly brown around edges. Remove from oven and place on a wire rack to cool.

Filing

Mix eggs and sugar until smooth. Add lemon juice and zest, stir. Fold in the flour. Pour the filling over hte crust and bake for 17-20 minutes, or until filling has set. Remove from oven and place on wire rack to cool.

Serve

Cut into squares and dust with powdered sugar. These are best eaten fresh. Store leftovers in refrigerator, covered, for 1-2 days max.

Pair

Goes great with a refreshing taste of green tea!
